Campaign Impact

Paid advertising drove significant traffic volume in September. When Apple/Meta campaigns stopped on October 7th, daily sessions dropped from ~18K to ~4K. Further decline to ~2.5K after October 15th when Google campaigns ended.

üì±

Mobile Dominance

Mobile devices account for 81.6% of all web sessions (529.5K sessions). iOS users show a higher bounce rate (22.3%) compared to Android (16.6%), suggesting potential optimization opportunities for iOS experience.

üåê

Organic Social Power

Organic Social became the dominant traffic source with 405.6K sessions (62.3% of total). TikTok Colombia (133.8K) and TikTok Mexico (97.5K) are the top performers, demonstrating strong organic brand presence.

‚ú®

High Engagement

Despite the traffic drop, engagement rate remained exceptionally high at 92.5%, with an average of 93.5 seconds per session. This suggests that remaining traffic is highly qualified and interested.

Geographic Focus

Colombia and Mexico campaigns drove 336.6K sessions combined (65.7% of identified campaigns). The Colombia market shows particularly strong engagement with 206.5K sessions.

üé®

Content Performance

Most viewed sections: English homepage (98.8K views), Colombia products (65.9K), and International money transfer (65.9K). Venezuela remittance pages show strong engagement with 27.3K views.
